Instructions for sharing community groups.

When you share a group, you are providing access to the group’s history plus new messages that come in.

Community groups are groups that:

  • have at least six members.
  • contain broadcasts more than chats.
  • are not chats with between family and close friends.
  • include local news, opportunities, campaigns, and political party groups.

No group messages will be collected until you have had a chance to confirm your group choices.

You can stop sharing groups at any time.
